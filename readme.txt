== Description ==

ShopMagic for Contact Form 7 is a free WooCommerce email plugin. It is an add-on to [ShopMagic](https://wordpress.org/plugins/shopmagic-for-woocommerce/) (which is also free). This plugin lets you use Contact Form 7 submissions directly in email marketing automation in your WooCommerce store or WordPress. For example, you can add a CF7 WordPress user to the MailChimp list or create follow-up emails, to the email entered in the contact form, for WooCommerce customers that had sent a product enquiry through Contact Form 7.

The add-on is a great integration between Contact Form 7, WordPress, and WooCommerce because it lets you boost your email marketing strategy with custom checkout, product inquiry, or adjusted contact forms. The ShopMagic for Contact Form 7 and WooCommerce integration is a **powerful plugin when you need automated newsletters, reminders, and follow-up emails** (like AutomateWoo) but also together with CF7! Read more about the [add-on settings](https://docs.shopmagic.app/article/1121-shopmagic-for-contact-form-7).

**ShopMagic for Contact Form 7 and WooCommerce is a free integration plugin**. Combine WooCommerce, Contact Form 7, and ShopMagic plugins to build **successful email marketing in WordPress and WooCommerce** today 🚀!

== Installation ==

1. Install either via the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the files to your server.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Go to WooCommerce &rarr; Automations in your WordPress admin area and add automation.
4. Choose an event and an action for automation based on your preferences.
5. Use the Contact Form 7 Submission Event and choose the contact form from CF7.
6. Decide about filters for user segmentation or leave the event for all users or customers.
7. Choose one or several Actions to meet your marketing strategy (send emails, add users to Mailchimp, send SMS notifications, or add data from WordPress and WooCommerce to Google Sheets).
